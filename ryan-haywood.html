    Ryan Haywood Professional Life

    Ryan’s professional journey began in high school. As stated in Let’s Play – 7 Days to Die Part 3, he used to work as a professional model. Aside from that, he has fronted many magazines and even done runway shows.

    Ryan found a job opening with Rooster Teeth on Craigslist in 2011, after moving to Corpus Christi, Texas. He applied and was invited to relocate to Austin. Similarly, he began working as a full-time editor for Achievement Hunter there.

    He was in charge of the Rooster Teeth website and the Game Fails YouTube channel. Following that, he worked as an editor, crew member, and recurring host before taking over as the main host in videos in March of 2012.

    Ryan claims that the first video he made was the NYC tour video, titled “A video game tour of NYC,” which Rooster Teeth uploaded to YouTube on June 10, 2011. Likewise, his voice was first heard in the video “The Witcher 2: Librarian Achievement Guide.”

As a result, he was promoted to the main crew members. Later that year, he was also named technical director and animator for Season 10 of Red vs. Blue.
