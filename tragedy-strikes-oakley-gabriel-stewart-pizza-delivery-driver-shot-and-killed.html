    In a shocking incident that has left the community of Oakley, California in mourning, 24-year-old pizza delivery driver Gabriel Stewart was shot and killed on Wednesday.

    The Contra Costa County Coroner’s Office has confirmed Stewart’s identity. Reports indicate that the young man was sitting in his car, taking a break from delivering pizzas when the fatal shooting occurred.

    Oakley Police Department, who are investigating the case, described the area where the incident took place as a ‘relatively quiet’ part of city. The sudden and brutal nature of the crime has shocked residents and left them seeking answers.

    Gabriel Stewart was known to be a hardworking individual, dedicated to his job. His untimely death is a loss not just to his family and friends, but also to the Domino’s Pizza team where he worked.

    As the investigation continues, the community stands united in their grief, while hoping for swift justice. This tragic event serves as a stark reminder of the dangers faced by delivery drivers and other frontline workers, even in seemingly safe neighborhoods.

    We extend our deepest condolences to Gabriel’s family, friends, and colleagues during this incredibly difficult time.
