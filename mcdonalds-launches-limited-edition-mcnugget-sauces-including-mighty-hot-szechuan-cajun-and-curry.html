    McDonald's launches limited-edition McNugget sauces including Mighty Hot, Szechuan, Cajun and Curry

    McDonalds is set to release two more limited-edition McNugget sauces as part of its winter menu. The popular fast food eatery announced itwill be adding a Curry Sauce and Mighty Hot Sauce to its menu from tomorrow July 20.

    16 Jun 2024, 00:00

    McDonald’s is set to release two more limited-edition McNugget sauces as part of its winter menu.

    The popular fast food eatery announced it will be adding a Curry Sauce and Mighty Hot Sauce to its menu from tomorrow July 20.

    The news comes as part of McDonald’s ‘Sauces in July’ promotion which gives Aussies diners a taste of dips from around the world. 

    Mc Donald has launched four limited edition McNugget sauces including Curry and Mighty Hot Sauces

    Previously, McDonald’s caused excitement among fans for bringing back the popular Cajun and Szechuan dipping sauces which have nearly sold out.

    Customers better be quick to try the new sauces as they are only available in stores while stocks last or until Tuesday, August 2. 

    Aussies can sample the British favourite curry sauce which is a permanent fixture on the UK’s Maccas menu and tastes ‘delicately spiced and fruity’. 

    Aussies can sample the British favourite curry sauce which is a permanent fixture on the UK’s Maccas menu and spice lovers will want to try the Mighty Hot that is packed with chilli

    Spice lovers will want to try the Mighty Hot dipping sauce as it ‘packs a punch’, taking chilli to the next level. 

    McDonald’s quietly dropped some new, fan-favourite and returning items to its winter menu earlier this month.  

    Launched nationwide, the eatery’s winter menu now includes mozzarella sticks and the first-ever Crème Brulée McFlurry and Crème Brulée Pie. 

    McDonald’s is bringing back its highly-popular Mighty Angus Burger and mozzarella sticks as part of its new winter menu

    Foodies can order the new and returning menu items now but you better be quick as they will only be available for a limited time. 

    The highly-popular Mighty Angus burger had a short hiatus from the menu and has been brought back due to strong demand.

    It has two 100 per cent Aussie Angus beef patties with chunky tomato relish, cheese bacon and red onions in a soft bun.

    Inspired by the classic dessert, the decadent new crème brulée McFlurry features vanilla ice-cream topped with a warm caramel drizzle and crispy pastry bites filled with a smooth custard and burnt caramel.

    Launching nationwide, the eatery’s winter menu will also include the first-ever Crème Brulée McFlurry and Crème Brulée Pie

    The crème brulée pie puts a twist on the French sweet treat with the same custard and caramel filling wrapped in delicious pastry and is served hot to warm customers this winter.

    Snack-lovers will want to try the crunchy mozzarella sticks served with a chunky tomato relish.

    The winter menu can be ordered via the MyMacca’s app and McDelivery or in selected stores across the country.
