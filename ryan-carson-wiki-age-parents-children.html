    Who is Ryan Carson?

    Ryan Carson, a renowned advocate for public policy on various issues like climate change and damage reduction, also dabbled in poetry.

    He penned a poem titled “Anxiety,” addressing his worries about death and the possible troubles it might bring to others.

    According to his LinkedIn page, Carson relocated from the Boston region to New York City in 2010. He did this to attend Pratt Institute.

    Afterward, he took on the role of senior solid waste campaign manager at the New York Public Interest Research Group (NYPIRG).

    He actively promotes sustainability and environmental policy.

    Suspect arrested in fatal stabbing of NY activist: Reports

    Multiple news outlets have reported that the police arrested a suspect in the fatal stabbing of New York City activist Ryan Carson.

    Carson was killed in front of his girlfriend in Brooklyn, New York.

    How did Ryan Carson die? 

    After attending a wedding the night before, Ryan Carson and his fiance were waiting at a bus stop in New York City when he was brutally stabbed to death.
