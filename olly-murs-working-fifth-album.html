    Olly Murs Working On Fifth Album

    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    We loved Olly Murs’ latest album ‘Never Been Better’ that was released back in 2014, and if you haven’t listened to it yet, check out our article about the whole album here.

    After publishing his book ‘On The Road’ Olly released a special edition of his ‘Never Been Better’ album, which included 7 extra tracks – including the new Murs-singles ‘Stevie Knows’ and ‘Kiss Me’-  besides the standard songs, and he released a Tour DVD that was recorded live at London’s famous O2 Arena.

    In addition to all of his success, the golden-throated singer is lucky to join Take That, Ella Eyre, Jamie Lawson and Nathan Sykes on 9th July in Hyde Park at the British Summer Time, presenting by Barclaycard.

    The singer who was more than busy in 2015 – because of his tour and The X-Factor’s UK version last year where he was one of the hosts – is now heading to the studio again to start working on his next album.

    In the last few days, Olly announced the news on his Twitter and Instagram.

    Now all of his fans and CelebMix are looking forward to hearing more about Murs’ 5th album!
