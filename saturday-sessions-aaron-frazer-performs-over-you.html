    Saturday Sessions: Aaron Frazer performs Over You&quot;

    Brooklyn-based and Baltimore-raised, Aaron Frazer made his mark as a multi-instrumentalist and singer with the soul revival band Durand Jones &amp; The Indications. He joined Saturday Sessions to share new songs out of his solo debut album appropriately titled Introducing. From Brooklyn, New York, Frazer performs Over You.
