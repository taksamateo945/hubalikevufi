    02 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Kourtney Kardashian’s 40th birthday is right around the corner (April 18) and the milestone seems to be catching her without a man in her life. Does she have any regrets regarding her lack of a love life lately?

    While Kourt is the first one to hit the big 40 out of her sisters, since she is the oldest, it turns out that for her, age means nothing – just a number, guys!

    That being said, the reality TV star is not freaking out over turning 40 years old at all!

    A source tells HollywoodLife that ‘Kourtney is in a great place and she’s not looking for love but if it happens, then she is open to the possibility of being in a relationship. Right now she is just focused on her kids, working out, and the launch of Poosh. Kourtney looks and feels hotter than ever, so she is feeling good about turning 40 and has no regrets.’

    She and her ex Scott Disick have been having a great co-parenting relationship so she is happy both in her personal life as well as in her career since just a few days ago, Kourtney launched her own Poosh website – all about wellness, fashion, beauty, and lifestyle.

    While a guest alongside sisters Khloe and Kim on Jimmy Kimmel Live on April 1, the oldest of the bunch hinted at how she was planning on celebrating her birthday.

    She revealed that at the party there will be nothing with ‘40’ on it and that includes balloons, napkins, or any decoration!

    The reason? – It’s just cheesy, so she asked her sisters to avoid displaying the number anywhere at the bash.
