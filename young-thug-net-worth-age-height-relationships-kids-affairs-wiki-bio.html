    Who is Young Thug?

    The rising hip-hop star has already released 18 mixtapes since he first entered the music scene in 2010; some of his most successful include “Barter 6” in 2015, then “I’m Up” in 2016, “Beautiful Thunder Girls” and “Super Slimey” both in 2017, the latter a collaboration with rapper Future.     Young Thug Wiki: Age, Early Life, and Education

    Born Jeffrey Lamar Williams on the 16th August 1991, in Atlanta, Georgia USA, he is the second youngest of 11 children, and spent his childhood in Atlanta’s housing projects, Jonesboro South, which is known for also spawning such rappers as Waka Flocka Flame, Ludacris, PeeWee Longway and 2 Chainz among others. Young Thug wasn’t just expelled from school when he was in the sixth grade after he broke teacher’s arm, but he also was sent to a juvenile prison where he spent the next four years.

    Career Beginnings

    Young Thug emerged on the music scene in 2010, when he worked with rapper TruRoyal on the song “She Can Go”. His next projects were three mixtapes “I Came from Nothing”, which were released through 2011 and 2012. His music received good critiques, and he was noticed by one of the Atlanta-based rapper’s – Gucci Mane – who quickly signed him to his own label 1017 Black Squad Records, which is under the Asylum/Atlantic imprint.  In 2013, Young Thug introduced himself to the wider public with the mixtape “1017 Thug”, which received further great reviews, and Rolling Stone magazine even placed it at No. 5 on their 10 Best Mixtapes of 2013. He continued to work hard on his music, and new collaborations followed, including with A$AP Ferg and Freddie Gibbs on the song “Old English” in 2014, then with Gucci Mane on “Panoramic Roof”, with T.I. on “About the Money”, “Lifestyle” with Rich Gang and Rich Homie Quan, all in 2014, and “Constantly Hating”, which featured Birdman, released in 2015.

    Rise to Prominence

    Gradually Young Thug’s career improved, becoming more popular with every new mixtape, as his fan base increased. His first commercial mixtape was “Barter 6” in 2015, followed by “I’m Up”, “Slime Season 3”, and “Jeffrey”, all in 2016, then “Beautiful Thugger Girls”, and “Super Slimey” in 2017.

    He has won several awards, including the AMA in categories Collaboration of the Year, Video of the Year, and Favorite Song Pop/Rock for his work on the song “Havana” with Camila Cabello.

    All of this has made Young Thug a new hip-hop star, and with new songs such as “The Weekend” and “Anybody”, has cemented his place among the successful Atlanta-based rappers.

    Young Thug Personal Life, Wife, Marriage, Children

    Young Thug has been engaged to swimsuit designer Jerrika Karlae since April 2015. The couple hasn’t shared details in regards to their future, so we can’t tell you if and when Young Thug is going to marry his fiancé. Nevertheless, Young is already a father of six, three boys and three daughters, with four different women.

    Young Thug and his fiancé reside in a Buckhead, Atlanta 11,000 square-feet home, which has six bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, a full bar and a theater room, as well as a four-car garage.

    Problems with the Law

    Ever since his teenage years, Young Thug has had problems with the law – probably that’s how he came up with the nickname? Back in 2015, he faced a lawsuit from a tour bus driver when his bus, on which Lil Wayne was riding, was attacked by members of the street-gang Bloods. Young Thug, Burdman, Cash Money Record, and Young Money Records were all in the lawsuit. In 2017 a lawsuit was filed against him after he failed to appear at a concert in Sahlen’s Stadium, for which he received $55,000. Later the same year, he was arrested for drug possession, and also for possession of a firearm. Though he was released, a year later,he was arrested yet again, this time for possession of and intent to distribute meth, hydrocodone and marijuana, and also for having a firearm with him.
