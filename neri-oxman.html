    Educational Background

    According to Neri Oxman’s biography on her official website, she received her Ph.D. in Design Computation at MIT in 2010.

    Career Background

    Neri Oxman’s works have been featured as permanent collections across prominent museums including MoMA, SFMOMA, Centre Pompidou, MAK Museum of Applied Arts, FRAC Collection for Art and Architecture, and the Cooper Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museum.

    She has over 150 scientific publications and inventions under her belt. Back in 2018, she was honored with the Cooper Hewitt National Design Award and the London Design Innovation Medal.

    In 2019, Oxman received an Honorary Fellowship by the Royal Institute of British Architects and the Contemporary Vision Award by the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art.
