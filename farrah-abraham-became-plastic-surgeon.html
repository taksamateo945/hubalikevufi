    Last week, MTV Teen Mom, Farrah Abraham announced her latest plan to becoming a plastic surgeon. According to Farrah, the last several years has taught her that she can achieve anything she wants, and she believes that plastic surgery is her calling.  During an interview with E!, Farrah stated: ” I really want to get my doctorate and I want to do plastic surgery. So that’s just something on top of all of the other things I want to do but that would probably be my end goal.”  While many were immediately skeptical of her latest scheme, Farrah seems to be entirely serious.  Although the chances of her actually becoming a plastic surgeon are slim to none, the idea itself certainly raises a lot of interesting thoughts.  Here are five things that would happen if Farrah Abraham became a plastic surgeon.

    5. Farrah Abraham would probably try to be her own patient.

    Over the years Farrah has proven to be extremely self-centered.  Her own track record with plastic surgery proves that she certainly isn’t above altering her appearance.  If Farrah were allowed to perform plastic surgery on anyone, there’s a good chance she would probably try to perform some sort of procedure on herself.

    4. Farrah Abraham would probably make her daughter a patient.

    In 2013, Farrah made headlines when she revealed that she had waxed her four-year old daughter’s eyebrows.  While there’s no denying that Farrah’s daughter is an absolutely adorable little girl, there’s also no denying that Farrah can be downright crazy.  It’s not hard to imagine plastic surgeon Farrah performing a minor procedure on her daughter, and then trying to rationalize it as a way to boost the young girl’s self image.

    3. Farrah Abraham would write a tell-all book.

    Since becoming a reality TV star, Farrah has proven that she’s willing to do almost anything to make a buck.  With that being said, doctor patient confidentiality is likely to go out of the window if Farrah feels like there’s money to be made by writing a tell-all book about her patients.

    2. Farrah Abraham would get her own reality TV series.

    Watching Farah on Teen Mom is pretty entertaining, but a show following her work as a plastic surgeon would definitely take the cake.

    1. The number of plastic surgery mishaps would certainly rise.

    Unfortunately, botched plastic surgery procedures happen relatively often.  However, with Farrah in charge of the operating room, the changes of plastic surgery mishaps definitely seems likely to increase.
